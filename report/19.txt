INFO

Popeye is a utility that scans live Kubernetes clusters and reports potential issues with

deployed resources and configurations. It sanitizes your cluster based on what's deployed
and not what's sitting on the disk. Scanning your cluster helps detect misconfigurations
and helps you to ensure that best practices are in place, thus preventing future
headaches.
